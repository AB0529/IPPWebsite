What Inovation Pathways is about?

Inovation Pathways is a worcester public schools hosted program that allows kids interested in trades such as Computer Science and IT, Construction, Health Care, And Business have an oppurtinity to learn and explore there carrers.

What are the benefits of inovation pathways?

Benefits of the Inovation Pathways Program are that you have an oppurtunity to learn more about a trade or carrer you are interested in and get real world experience through internships.
