Welcome to Requirement Page!

The requirements for the first year are completing the 10 hour OSHA certification.
Another requirement for the first year is having a summer internship. Throughout the course of an IPP student's high school career, they are required to take two college level courses, either at a local college or Advanced Placement.
For the second year you will be required to take a internship or do a capstone project.
