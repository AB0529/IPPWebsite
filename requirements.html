The requirements for the first year would be that you will have to complete Osha and that will take you 10 hours to finish. Another requirement for the first year would be taking a internship. You also will be taking 2 college class courses in your school and that will be for 2 quarters. For the second year you will be required to take a internship or do a capstone project for the summer and you also be taking the 2 college class courses for 2 quarters at your school.
